SchemeSetu AI is a privacy-first trust wallet and AI-powered scheme discovery platform designed for India's 450 million+ informal workers — street vendors, daily wage earners, gig workers, and domestic helpers.

These workers lack formal income documentation (pay slips, tax returns, bank statements), making them unable to access the 1,400+ government welfare schemes they may be eligible for. SchemeSetu AI solves this by providing:

  • A Document Trust Wallet with OCR-powered verification and blockchain-style hashing
  • AI-driven scheme matching against 1,476 real government schemes from myScheme.gov.in
  • Verifiable income reports with QR codes and SHA-256 hash chains
  • A multilingual AI chatbot supporting 10+ Indian languages via voice and text
  • Public verification — banks and government offices can verify reports without login
Mission: Bridge the gap between India's informal economy and government welfare systems — enabling financial inclusion through verifiable digital identity, in every Indian language.

2. Problem Statement

The Financial Exclusion Crisis

India's informal sector constitutes 83% of the workforce (ILO estimates). These workers face a compounding cycle of exclusion:

❌ Challenges Workers Face

  • No formal income proof or employment records
  • Cannot prove eligibility for government schemes
  • Denied micro-loans and financial services
  • Language barriers — most apps are English-only
  • Low digital literacy — complex registration processes
  • Fear of data misuse by intermediaries

❌ Systemic Gaps

  • 1,400+ schemes across central & state governments
  • Fragmented portals — no single discovery platform
  • No standardized way to verify informal income
  • CSC operators lack digital tools for verification
  • No tamper-proof audit trail for income claims
  • No privacy-preserving identity framework
The Core Problem: There is no trusted, verifiable, and privacy-preserving way for an informal worker to prove their income — and no platform that matches them with the government schemes they deserve.

How it works:

  1. Sign In — Workers authenticate using their phone number via OTP (Amazon SNS). No email, no passwords, no app downloads needed.
  2. Upload Documents — Upload invoices, receipts, bank statements, Aadhaar, PAN cards. Amazon Textract performs OCR extraction. Each document is SHA-256 hashed and recorded in an immutable ledger.
  3. Discover Schemes — AI matches the worker's profile and documents against 1,476 real government schemes, showing eligibility percentages, match reasons, and missing requirements.
  4. Generate Report — Produces tamper-proof income verification reports with blockchain-style hash chains and QR codes. Downloadable as PDF. Publicly verifiable by banks and government offices.

4. Key Features

4.1 Document Trust Wallet

  • Drag-and-drop upload supporting PDF, PNG, JPG, CSV (up to 10MB)
  • Document type classification: invoice, receipt, bank statement, Aadhaar, PAN, other
  • Automated OCR via Amazon Textract — extracts date, amount, vendor, category
  • OCR confidence scoring for each extracted field
  • SHA-256 hash generation for tamper detection
  • Immutable ledger recording in DynamoDB
  • Three-state verification: Unverified → Pending Review → Verified
  • Server-side encryption with AWS KMS

4.2 AI Scheme Discovery

  • 1,476 real government schemes from myScheme.gov.in
  • Profile-based matching: occupation, state, district, income level, age
  • Document-based matching: checks if required documents are uploaded
  • Eligibility scoring with percentage breakdown
  • Match reasons (green checkmarks): "Income criteria met," "State eligibility matched"
  • Missing requirements (orange warnings): "Upload income proof," "Update occupation"
  • Document Match %: (matched documents / required documents) × 100
  • Direct "Apply Now" links to myScheme.gov.in

4.3 Multilingual AI Chatbot

  • Powered by Amazon Bedrock (Nova Micro model)
  • Context-aware — receives user's profile, documents, and top 10 eligible schemes
  • Text and voice input support
  • Voice transcription via Sarvam AI (saarika v2.5) — supports: Hindi, Tamil, Telugu, Kannada, Bengali, Gujarati, Marathi, Punjabi, Odia, Malayalam, English
  • Auto-translation of AI responses to user's selected language via Sarvam Translate API
  • Interactive scheme cards embedded in chat responses
  • Suggested action buttons for common queries

4.4 Verifiable Income Reports

  • Date range selection for report period
  • Aggregates all verified documents in the period
  • Calculates total income + average monthly income
  • Computes fresh Trust Score with 4-factor breakdown
  • SHA-256 hash chain — each report's hash includes previous report's hash (blockchain-style immutable ledger)
  • QR code generation linking to public verification portal
  • PDF download with branding, income summary, trust score, and QR code

4.5 Public Verification Portal

  • No authentication required — accessible by anyone
  • Two verification modes: Report ID or Blockchain Hash
  • Displays: verification status, trust score, income summary, document count
  • QR code scanning support
  • Designed for banks, government offices, and employers to verify income claims

4.6 CSC Operator Portal

  • Queue-based document verification interface for India's 500,000+ Common Service Centers
  • Pending/Completed tabs with real-time statistics
  • Document review modal with Approve/Reject decisions and verifier notes
  • Human verification layer adds credibility to automated OCR

4.7 Trust Score System

  • Composite score from 4 weighted factors (detailed in Section 12)
  • Transparent breakdown with progress bars
  • Color-coded: Green (≥80%), Yellow (60-79%), Red (<60%)
  • Updates dynamically as documents are uploaded and verified

4.8 Privacy & Consent

  • GDPR-style explicit consent collection before any data processing
  • Two separate consent toggles: Data Processing + Scheme Matching
  • Consent is revocable anytime from Settings
  • Right to be Forgotten — complete account deletion with confirmation
  • Server-side API key handling — Sarvam AI key never exposed to browser

12. Trust Score Algorithm

The Trust Score is a composite metric (0-100) that quantifies the credibility of a worker's financial identity. It uses 4 weighted factors:

FactorWeightWhat It MeasuresHow It's Calculated
Document Quality40%Completeness and authenticity of uploaded documentsAverage OCR confidence × verified document ratio × document count bonus
Income Stability30%Consistency of income over timeInverse of income variance across documents. Lower variance = higher score
Recency15%How recent the documents areWeighted average of document ages. Documents <30 days = full score, decays over time
Source Reliability15%Type and trustworthiness of document sourcesWeighted by doc type: Bank statement (100%) > Invoice (80%) > Receipt (60%) > Other (40%)
Formula: Trust Score = (Doc Quality × 0.40) + (Income Stability × 0.30) + (Recency × 0.15) + (Source Reliability × 0.15)

Color coding: Green (≥80, Excellent) • Yellow (60-79, Good) • Orange (40-59, Fair) • Red (<40, Needs Improvement)

The Trust Score is displayed with full transparency — users see exactly how each factor contributes, and what actions can improve their score (upload more docs, get verified by CSC, upload recent docs).

13. Blockchain-Style Verification

SchemeSetu uses a SHA-256 hash chain stored in DynamoDB's immutable ledger table to ensure tamper-proof verification:

How It Works

  1. Document Upload: When a document is uploaded, a SHA-256 hash of its content + metadata is generated using deterministic key-sorted JSON serialization (stableStringify)
  2. Ledger Entry: The hash, along with the previous entry's hash, is recorded in the Ledger table — creating a chain
  3. Report Generation: When an income report is generated, a new hash is computed from the report data + all contributing document hashes + the previous report's hash
  4. Verification: Any verifier can recompute the hash from the report data and compare it against the stored hash. If any data was tampered with, the hash won't match
